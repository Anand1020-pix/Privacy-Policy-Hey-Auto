Privacy Policy for Hey Auto

Effective Date: 08/11/2024

Your privacy is important to us at Hey Auto. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you share with us through the Hey Auto mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect certain personally identifiable information, including:

  • Contact Information: such as your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account.
  • Location Information: We collect real-time location data only when you use the app to book or track rides.

b. Usage Data

We gather information on how you interact with the app, including error reports, interaction data, and performance metrics.

c. Device Information

We may collect information about the device used to access Hey Auto, such as model, operating system, and IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Respond to customer inquiries and provide support.
  • Send notifications about your rides and account.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that assist us with functions such as mapping and notification delivery.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or necessary to protect our users and operations.

4. Data Security

We employ reasonable measures to safeguard your information. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the complete security of your data.

5. Your Rights

You may have rights related to accessing, updating, or deleting your information, depending on your location. To exercise these rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes listed in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Hey Auto does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
